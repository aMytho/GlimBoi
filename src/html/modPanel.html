Mod History

Add Banned Words

Enter the banned words to add. You can separate multiple words with spaces. 1 word per trigger (ie. "What's Twitch?" would have 2 triggers, not 1 phrase)

Remove Banned Words

Enter the banned words to remove. You can separate multiple words with space. 1 word per trigger (ie. "What's Twitch?" would remove 2 words, not 1 phrase)

Toggle Filter

Filter Status: Disabled

Disabling the filter will prevent the bot from scanning messages for banned words.

Enabling the filter will cause the bot to follow the warnings that are set.

Reset Filter

Resetting the filter will clear all of your added/removed words. It will be reset to its default state. This is irreversible.
