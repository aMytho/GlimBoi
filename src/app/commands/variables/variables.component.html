Variables are inserted into the action ChatMessage as $NAME . They are replaced with specific data as listed below.
Name Command Data Example Command Result Explanation
$target Go follow $target at Glimesh.tv/$target !so Mytho | Go follow Mytho at Glimesh.tv/Mytho The first word after the command.
$message You just said: $message !parrot Hello there | You just said: Hello there Everything after the command.
$user $user looked in the mirror and saw $user !mirror | Mytho looked in the mirror and saw Mytho The user who used the command
$rank Your rank is $rank !status | Mytho is rank Streamer The rank of the user
$watchtime You have watched the stream for $watchtime minutes !hours | You have watched the stream for 60 minutes The amount of time the user has watched the stream (15 min blocks)
$cmdcount This command has been used $cmdcount times !count | This command has been used 5 times The amount of times the command has been used
$advice Have some advice - $advice !advice | Have some advice: Drink some water Random piece of advice. May go over 255 characters
$catfact Random fact about cats: $catfact !catfact | Random fact about cats: Cats have 230 bones. Returns a random cat fact. May go over 255 characters.
$dogfact Random fact about dogs: $dogfact !dogfact | Random fact about dogs: Your dog can be left or right-pawed. Returns a random dog fact. May go over 255 characters.
$dadjoke Funny joke: $dadjoke !joke | I was fired from the keyboard factory yesterday. I wasn't putting in enough shifts. Returns a random dad joke. May go over 255 characters
$discord Join our discord at $discord !discord | Join our discord at https://discord.com/invite/NZeqEjhHKf Returns the channels discord invite URL. Must be in the Glimesh API.
$guilded Join our guilded at $guilded !guilded | Join our guilded at https://guided.gg/test Returns the channels guilded invite URL. Must be in the Glimesh API.
$instagram Follow me on instagram at $instagram !instagram | Follow me on instagram at https://instagram.con/test Returns the channels instagram URL. Must be in the Glimesh API.
$youtube Join our yotuube channel at $youtube !yt | Join our youtube channel at https://youtube.com/test Returns the channels youtube URL. Must be in the Glimesh API.
$twitter Follow me on twitter at $twitter !twitter | Follow me on twitter at https://twitter.com/Glimboi Returns the channels twitter URL. Must be in the Glimesh API.
$uptime You have been streaming for $uptime minutes! !uptime | You have been streaming for 100 minutes! Returns how long the streamer has been live in minutes.
$donationAmount (Donation Trigger) Someone donated $donationAmount. Someone donates | Someone donated $5.00. Returns the donation amount.
$recipient (GiftSub Trigger) $recipient was just Gifted a sub! Someone gifts a sub | Mytho was just gifted a sub Returns who was gifted a sub.
