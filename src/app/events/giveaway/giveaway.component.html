Giveaway

A Giveaway picks 1 user who entered to "win". This is different from raffles in that it does not reward any points.

A Giveaway can be started on this page or by typing !giveaway in chat. Users type !enter to join the Giveaway.

The results will be updated live on this page and the winner will be announced to chat.
